Who I Am

Bridging science,
business & customer insight.

I'm a marketing and strategy professional with a rare combination of food technology expertise and commercial leadership experience. Born and raised in Tamil Nadu, India, I've always been fascinated by how products, businesses, and people come together to create value.

After completing a B.Tech in Food Technology & Management at NIFTEM — one of India's leading food science institutions — I joined AAK India, a Swedish multinational specialising in specialty oils and fats. Over three years, I managed 30+ B2B accounts, drove 2,500+ MT in commercial volume, and was recognised as a top-performing sales professional.

Today, I'm channelling that commercial experience into an MSc Marketing & Strategy at Warwick Business School, sharpening my expertise in consumer behaviour, market research, digital strategy, and business analytics. I'm also Partnerships Lead at TEDxWarwick and contribute to consulting projects through Warwick Consulting Society.

My goal is a career at the intersection of marketing, strategy, and commercial leadership — where analytical thinking meets genuine customer empathy.

The Story So Far

My Journey

2000

Tamil Nadu, India

Where curiosity took root.

Growing up in Tamil Nadu, I was surrounded by a culture of food, trade, and entrepreneurship. I was endlessly curious about how things were made, how businesses worked, and what made products succeed in the market. That curiosity never left me.

2018

NIFTEM, Sonipat — Delhi NCR

Science meets management.

I moved from Tamil Nadu to Delhi NCR to pursue a B.Tech in Food Technology & Management at NIFTEM — one of India's most respected food science institutions. Here I learned food processing, quality systems, manufacturing, and product development, while simultaneously developing as a leader: captaining the volleyball team, representing students as Year Representative, and competing nationally.

2021

Hindustan Coca-Cola Beverages

First taste of corporate operations.

A formative internship at one of India's most iconic FMCG operations. Working on root cause analysis of PET bottle rejection and water optimisation in the Effluent Treatment Plant, I saw firsthand how rigorous quality thinking underpins great consumer brands. It was here I realised I wanted to connect the technical with the commercial.

2022

AAK India Pvt. Ltd. — Mumbai & across India

Three years of commercial growth.

Joining AAK India as a Technical Sales Officer was the turning point. Over three years, I managed 30+ B2B accounts across confectionery, bakery, and food manufacturing. I drove 2,500+ MT of commercial volume, commercialised solutions worth ~1.5M SEK in net margin, and presented to global leadership teams. I was recognised as Best Sales Employee and presented at AAK Global Kick-Off events in consecutive years. This role transformed my understanding of how businesses — and people — make decisions.

2025

Warwick Business School, Coventry, UK

Sharpening strategy at Warwick.

Trading Mumbai for Coventry, I enrolled in the MSc Marketing & Strategy at one of Europe's top business schools. Here I'm building on my commercial experience with rigorous frameworks in consumer behaviour, market research, digital strategy, and business analytics. Outside the classroom, I lead corporate partnerships at TEDxWarwick and contribute to consulting projects — including an engagement with DPD analysing cost efficiencies. I also led a team to Global 2nd Runner-Up at the NYIT International Dispute Resolution Competition, the only Indian team in the global Top 4.

2026
